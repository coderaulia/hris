### **PT Warna Emas Indonesia** **2026 KPI Measurement Guide**

### **Demand and Brand Side**

#### **Client Leader**

* **Revenue Growth**  
  * **Metric:** Revenue Growth Rate  
  * **Calculation:** (Current Period Revenue minus Previous Period Revenue) divided by Previous Period Revenue.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Upsell Rate**  
  * **Metric:** Cross-sell/Upsell Conversion  
  * **Calculation:** (Number of existing clients purchasing new services divided by Total number of active clients) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Client Advocacy Score**  
  * **Metric:** Net Promoter Score (NPS)  
  * **Calculation:** Percentage of "Promoters" (score 9-10) minus Percentage of "Detractors" (score 0-6).  
  * **Unit:** Score (Integer between \-100 and 100\)  
* **Billing Accuracy and Timeliness**  
  * **Metric:** Perfect Billing Rate  
  * **Calculation:** (Number of invoices sent on time without errors divided by Total invoices sent) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)

#### **Planning and Analyst**

* **Budget Utilization Efficiency**  
  * **Metric:** Budget Variance  
  * **Calculation:** (Actual Spend divided by Approved Monthly Plan) multiplied by 100\. (Closer to 100% is ideal; significantly higher or lower indicates inefficiency).  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Resource Calibration Impact**  
  * **Metric:** Margin Improvement  
  * **Calculation:** (Project Margin After Calibration minus Project Margin Before Calibration) divided by Margin Before Calibration.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Market Intelligence Success**  
  * **Metric:** Strategy Win Count  
  * **Calculation:** Total count of pitches or campaigns won where data insights were cited as the key success factor.  
  * **Unit:** Number (Count)  
* **Forecast Accuracy**  
  * **Metric:** Forecast Deviation Rate  
  * **Calculation:** Absolute value of (Actual Revenue minus Forecasted Revenue) divided by Actual Revenue.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%) (Lower is better)

#### **New Business Development**

* **Lead Conversion Rate**  
  * **Metric:** Lead-to-Deal Ratio  
  * **Calculation:** (Number of Closed Deals divided by Number of Qualified Leads) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **New Revenue Contribution**  
  * **Metric:** New Business Revenue / GMV  
  * **Calculation:** Sum of Total Contract Value or GMV from new clients within the period.  
  * **Unit:** Rupiah (IDR)  
* **Commission Efficiency**  
  * **Metric:** Commission to Revenue Ratio  
  * **Calculation:** (Total Commissions Paid divided by Total Revenue/GMV Generated) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Pipeline Velocity**  
  * **Metric:** Average Sales Cycle Length  
  * **Calculation:** Average number of days from "Lead Identification" to "Contract Signed".  
  * **Unit:** Days

### **Implementation and Platform**

#### **Implementation (CM and PM)**

* **Delivery OTIF (On-Time In-Full)**  
  * **Metric:** OTIF Rate  
  * **Calculation:** (Number of campaigns delivered 100% complete by the deadline divided by Total campaigns delivered) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Optimization Frequency**  
  * **Metric:** Optimization Count  
  * **Calculation:** Total count of documented interventions/adjustments made to live campaigns.  
  * **Unit:** Number (Count)  
* **Financial Control**  
  * **Metric:** COGS Margin Adherence  
  * **Calculation:** (Actual COGS divided by Revenue) compared to (Target COGS divided by Revenue).  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Revenue Contribution**  
  * **Metric:** Business Revenue / GMV  
  * **Calculation:** Sum of Total Contract Value or GMV from managed clients within the period.  
  * **Unit:** Rupiah (IDR)

#### **Livestreaming**

* **BAU Performance**  
  * **Metric:** Average Daily GMV  
  * **Calculation:** Total GMV generated on non-campaign days divided by Number of non-campaign days.  
  * **Unit:** Rupiah (IDR)  
* **Big Sale Uplift**  
  * **Metric:** Campaign Uplift Factor  
  * **Calculation:** ((Big Sale GMV minus Average Daily GMV) divided by Average Daily GMV) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Audience Retention**  
  * **Metric:** Average View Duration  
  * **Calculation:** Total Time Watched by all viewers divided by Total Number of Views.  
  * **Unit:** Minutes/Seconds  
* **Conversion Efficiency**  
  * **Metric:** Click-to-Order Rate (CTOR)  
  * **Calculation:** (Total Orders divided by Total Product Clicks in stream) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)

### **Supply and Creator Side**

#### **WCA (WEI Creator Academy)**

* **Supply Growth**  
  * **Metric:** Net WCA Creator Growth  
  * **Calculation Process:** ((Total WCA Creators at End of Period minus Total WCA Creators at Start of Period) divided by Total WCA Creators at Start of Period) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Creator ROI**  
  * **Metric:** Return on Investment per Creator  
  * **Calculation Process:** (Total GMV Generated by WCA Creators divided by Total Investment in WCA Creators \[Training Costs \+ Management Fees\]) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%) or Ratio (e.g., 5x)  
* **Creator Utilization**  
  * **Metric:** Active Supply Count  
  * **Calculation Process:** Count of unique WCA creators who have been booked or utilized for at least one campaign or project within the period.  
  * **Unit:** Number (Count) of Creators  
* **Utilization Rate**  
  * **Metric:** Supply Efficiency Rate  
  * **Calculation Process:** (Number of Active WCA Creators \[from the metric above\] divided by Total Available WCA Creators in the Pool) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Income Revenue from Platform**  
  * **Metric:** Platform Incentive Revenue  
  * **Calculation Process:** Sum of all direct revenue earned from the platform (e.g., Mission rewards, Platform bonuses, Affiliate commissions paid by the app).  
  * **Unit:** Rupiah (IDR)

### **Creator Manager**

* **Growth of New Creators**  
  * **Metric:** Net Creator Acquisition Rate  
  * **Calculation Process:** ((Number of Active Creators at End of Period minus Number of Active Creators at Start of Period) divided by Number of Active Creators at Start of Period) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Growth of Creator Performance**  
  * **Metric:** Performance Improvement Rate (Views/Engagement)  
  * **Calculation Process:** ((Average Views or Engagement per Creator in Current Month minus Average in Previous Month) divided by Average in Previous Month) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Income Revenue from Platform**  
  * **Metric:** Platform Incentive Revenue  
  * **Calculation Process:** Sum of all direct revenue earned from the platform (e.g., Mission rewards, Platform bonuses, Affiliate commissions paid by the app).  
  * **Unit:** Rupiah (IDR)  
* **Inflation for Mid & Top Creator**  
  * **Metric:** Creator Cost Inflation Rate  
  * **Calculation Process:** ((Average Cost/Rate Card of Top & Mid Creators in Current Period minus Average Cost in Previous Period) divided by Average Cost in Previous Period) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **GMV/Creator**  
  * **Metric:** Average GMV per Creator Growth  
  * **Calculation Process:** First, calculate Average GMV (Total GMV divided by Total Active Creators). Then calculate growth: ((Current Average GMV minus Previous Average GMV) divided by Previous Average GMV) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)

### **Business Operation**

#### **Finance and Accounting**

* **Accrual Accuracy**  
  * **Metric:** Booking Variance  
  * **Calculation:** Absolute value of (Booked Revenue/Cost minus Actual Bank Statement Amount).  
  * **Unit:** Rupiah (IDR)  
* **Vendor Liability Management**  
  * **Metric:** On-Time Payment Rate  
  * **Calculation:** (Number of payments made within agreed terms divided by Total number of payments) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Forecasting Precision**  
  * **Metric:** Budget Variance Ratio  
  * **Calculation:** (Actual Financial Results minus Forecasted Results) divided by Forecasted Results.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Compliance and Reporting**  
  * **Metric:** Compliance Defect Rate  
  * **Calculation:** Number of late tax filings or internal audit flags.  
  * **Unit:** Number (Count)

#### **Finance Admin**

* **Document Processing Speed**  
  * **Metric:** Average Turnaround Time  
  * **Calculation:** Sum of hours spent processing documents divided by Total documents processed.  
  * **Unit:** Hours  
* **Data Entry Integrity**  
  * **Metric:** Data Error Rate  
  * **Calculation:** (Number of entries with detected errors divided by Total entries made) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%) (Lower is better)  
* **Expense Tracking**  
  * **Metric:** Opex Budget Utilization  
  * **Calculation:** (Actual Operational Expense divided by Budgeted Operational Expense) multiplied by 100\.  
  * **Unit:** Percentage (%)  
* **Support Responsiveness**  
  * **Metric:** Mean Time to Resolve (MTTR)  
  * **Calculation:** Average time taken to close internal finance tickets/queries.  
  * **Unit:** Hours
