HR Performance Suite Manual

Quick guide for daily use by Employee, Manager, and Super Admin.

Back to App
Tip: Features are role-based. If a tab is missing, your account role may not have access.

1. Login & Account

  • Enter your registered email and password, then select Sign In.
  • If needed, use Forgot Password on the login page to request a reset link.
  • After login, use Password in the top-right header to change your password.

2. Navigation Tabs

  • Dashboard: Summary views for assessment and KPI performance.
  • Employees: Employee data management (Super Admin only).
  • Assessment & KPI: Run performance assessments and KPI workflow.
  • Records: View assessment records, KPI history, and training logs.
  • Settings: App configuration, users, competencies, KPI definitions, and organization setup (Super Admin only).

3. Common Workflow

  1. Check data and updates in Dashboard.
  2. Select employee and run manager assessment in Assessment & KPI.
  3. Input KPI achievements for the same period in Assessment & KPI.
  4. Validate final data in Records (Assessment Records and KPI Records).
  5. Use exports for reporting and maintain setup in Settings (if authorized).

4. How To Do Assessment

  1. Open Assessment & KPI tab.
  2. From Select Employee, choose the employee to assess.
  3. Click Assess Competencies.
  4. Choose Position, fill competency scores/answers, then click Review Answers.
  5. Check results on Review Submission, then click Final Submit.
  6. Verify the saved result in Records > Assessment Records.

5. How To Input KPI Achievement

  1. Open Assessment & KPI tab and select employee.
  2. Click Input KPI Achievement.
  3. Select KPI Metric and Period.
  4. Enter KPI value:
    • For normal metrics, fill Final Value.
    • For percentage metrics, fill Attained and Total so percentage is calculated.
  5. Add optional notes, then click Submit Achievement.
  6. Verify the entry in Records > KPI Records.

6. Report Filters

  • Use top filters (Department, Manager, Period) to scope Dashboard and Records outputs.
  • Select Clear to reset all filters.

7. Exports & Imports

  • Export options are available in relevant modules (CSV, JSON, Excel, PDF).
  • Before importing files, verify format and required columns/fields.

8. Session & Security

  • Inactive sessions are logged out automatically after timeout.
  • Always log out from shared devices.

9. Troubleshooting

  • Data not visible: confirm your role and selected filters.
  • Cannot save/update: check required fields and network connection.
  • Access issue persists: contact your system administrator.
